# Code of Conduct

Hermes Pets should be a welcoming, practical place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions when something is unclear.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Be patient with new contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, or project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; block participants; or take other reasonable action to keep the project healthy.

To report a conduct concern, contact Tony Simons at tony@tonysimons.dev.
